*Use:* Technical materials, methodology documents, CIO/architect messaging

**"Assessments designed by strategists, executed by operators."**

*Use:* Assessment hub, specific service pages, sales enablement
*Why:* Emphasizes assessment-first model and execution capability

## Voice Checklist for All Copy

Before publishing any page copy, check:

- [ ] No buzzwords (innovative, synergistic, leverage, transform, empower)
- [ ] No vendor-speak (managed services, solutions, best-in-class, industry-leading)
- [ ] Frameworks mentioned (NIST, CIS, CSF visible where relevant)
- [ ] Problem-first (starts with buyer pain or goal)
- [ ] Outcome-focused (what improves, what changes)
- [ ] Specific not vague ("improve compliance posture by X score points" not "improve security")
- [ ] Active voice (we do X, not X is being done)
- [ ] Assumes technical competence (no hand-holding, no explaining industry terms)
- [ ] Clear CTAs (action verb + specific outcome)
- [ ] No hype or guarantees (realistic, measured claims)
